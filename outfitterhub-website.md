## 7. Site Vibe & Visual Direction

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Deep Forest Green (#1A3C34)
- **Secondary:** Burnt Orange (#D4773B) — accent
- **Neutral:** Charcoal (#2D2D2D), Cream (#F5F2EB)
- **Luxury:** Gold accents (#C9A961)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Rugged Serif (e.g., Gibson, Chord) — bold, classic
- **Body:** Clean Sans (e.g., Inter, Proxima Nova) — readable
- **Accents:** All-Caps Industrial (e.g., DIN, Freight Sans)

### Visual Style
- Full-bleed hero images (mountains, rivers, trophies)
- Video backgrounds (slow-mo casting, elk walking)
- Dark mode aesthetic — premium, moody
- Copper/bronze accents on CTAs
- Minimal UI — let the photography breathe

### Truck/Mobile Wrap Ideas
- Green truck with "OUTFITTERHUB" in gold lettering
- Mountains silhouette in background
- Website QR code on back panel
- Tagline: "Book the Hunt. Not the Guesswork."

## 8. Technical Recommendations

### Platform
- **Webflow** (premium feel, fast) or **WordPress** with custom theme
- Consider **Framer** for ultra-fast landing pages

### Must-Haves
- Booking inquiry form (not full e-commerce yet)
- Email capture on every page
- Blog with category filtering
- Trip search/filter (by species, location, price, season)
- High-res image library (budget for pro photography)

### Integrations
- Mailchimp or ConvertKit for email
- Calendly or custom booking form
- Analytics (GA4, GSC)
