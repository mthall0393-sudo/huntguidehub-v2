# 2026-03-28

## CompanyCam Opportunity Idea

**What is CompanyCam:**
- Photo documentation app for field workers (contractors, trades)
- Captures job site photos → organized feed
- AI-powered reports, checklists, payments
- Integrates with Jobber, ServiceTitan, AccuLynx

**Funding/Market:**
- $38M total raised (Series C Aug 2025)
- Investors: Insight Partners, Sweetwater PE
- Competitors: Beekeeper, OpenSpace, Orion, Raken

**Target industries:**
- HVAC, Landscaping, Roofing, Plumbing, Electrical, Restoration, General Contractors

**Why it's interesting:**
- Field documentation is hot space
- Dominated by CompanyCam but opens niche opportunities
- Could build AI-only tool for specific trades
- Or build lighter, cheaper alternative for small operators

**Competitor gaps:**
- No "lightweight" option for solo/small teams
- No specific focus on homeowners vs commercial
- AI features could be standalone
