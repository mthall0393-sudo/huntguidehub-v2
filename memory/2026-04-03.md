**Product:** After-hours AI voice agent for contractors
**Stack:** n8n + Bland.ai (or Retell.ai)
**Niche:** HVAC (highest need, least competition)

## Why This Won

- Higher price point ($150-300/mo) vs SMS ($49/mo)

- Contractor sets business hours (8am-6pm)

- Contractor gets notification + lead details
